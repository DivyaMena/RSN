<product_requirements>
The Rising Stars Nation web application is a multi-user platform for a non-profit that provides free online tuition.

**Core User Roles & Features:**
1.  **Users:** Parents, Students, Tutors, and Coordinators, all authenticating via Gmail (implemented via a test login system for now).
2.  **Registration:**
    *   **Parents:** Register their children, providing details like class, board, and subjects. Includes Aadhaar and photo upload for KYC.
    *   **Tutors:** Register to volunteer, specifying subjects, board preference, and availability. Registration requires coordinator approval and includes fields for address, a brief bio, and Aadhaar/photo uploads.
3.  **Batch Management:**
    *   Students are automatically pooled into batches (min 10, max 25) based on class and subject.
    *   Batch codes are generated sequentially.
    *   Tutors are assigned automatically, with coordinators able to override.
4.  **Log Board:**
    *   Tutors post what they taught in a class, including their Google Meet link.
    *   Entries are locked after posting to prevent edits by tutors.
    *   The board is visible to students and parents of that batch.
5.  **Dashboards:**
    *   **Parent:** View their children's progress and batch log boards.
    *   **Student:** View their own classes, log boards, and request remedial classes.
    *   **Tutor:** View assigned batches, manage log board entries, and see enrolled students.
    *   **Coordinator:** Manage tutors (approve/reject/blacklist), assign tutors to batches, view all batches, and see remedial class requests.
</product_requirements>
