<product_requirements>
The Rising Stars Nation application is a multi-role educational platform. The core goal is to build out comprehensive dashboards and registration flows for all user types, with a super-admin having ultimate control.

**Key Roles & Features:**
*   **RSN (Admin):** A super-admin role () with a dedicated dashboard to view and manage all platform data (users, batches, subjects, etc.). This role handles coordinator assignments and manages site-wide settings like curriculum and holidays. There is no public signup for this role.
*   **School:** Schools register with detailed information (board, address, classes taught, required subjects). After coordinator approval, they access a dashboard to specify their Tutors Unavailable timetable, indicating where they need volunteer teachers.
*   **Coordinator:** Manages tutors and batches. The dashboard includes complex filters, tutor assignment capabilities, and a view of pending approvals. They can request changes to their own availability, which must be approved by an Admin.
*   **Tutor:** Registers with detailed preferences (classes, subjects, preferred slots). The dashboard allows them to view assigned batches, manage their availability (with coordinator approval for preference changes), and post log board entries with strict Google Meet link validation.
*   **Parent/Student:** Parents register students with DOB and T&C acceptance. Students log in with their parent's email and their DOB. They can view batches, request remedial classes, and have their attendance marked by confirming Join Class.

A central, rule-based **Global Batch Schedule** must be implemented. For each batch, a schedule (e.g., 3-4 slots/week) is pre-generated based on class level and subject, respecting constraints like no consecutive slots on the same day. This schedule is visualized on the Coordinator dashboard with color-coded slots (Yellow for unassigned, Green for assigned).
</product_requirements>
